Registry ownership

Copy the component source or a brand pack into your own Angular codebase.

The local registry works like a shadcn-style handoff. Teams can keep consuming the published package, or copy the component source and a brand starter into their app and take responsibility for local edits there.

pnpm registry:list pnpm registry:add ff-button-source ./src/owned/components/ff-button pnpm registry:add owned-brand-pack ./src/owned/brands/registry-owned
registry/index.json registry/items/ff-button-source.json registry/items/owned-brand-pack.json examples/registry-installed/ff-button-source examples/registry-installed/owned-brand-pack

This repo already contains an installed sample pack under examples/registry-installed/owned-brand-pack, and the selector above can switch to Registry Owned immediately.
